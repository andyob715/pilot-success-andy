LIFT Background

The Leadership In Flight Training (LIFT) Academy offers pilot training programs including a direct classroom-to-cockpit opportunity. Working with Republic Airlines, LIFT Academy training has the potential to advance to Captain. The programs are tailored around each student’s schedule.

Project Goal: Predict Profitability of an Incoming Student

Using real data from Lift Academy to determine a student’s flight hours based on days spent within the course. This will be used to predict time requirements for new students and cost per student for the company. In the future, additional information can be added to this to determine how much time it will take a multicourse student to complete all necessary training to become a trainer or move to domestic/national flight captain.

Project Steps

Starting Point

Gathered all graduation data from LIFT Academy records and validated usability of data

Additional Data Construction

Removed/verified no PII was available in the dataset. Added/manipulated columns to get better inputs for prediction. Calculated whether student was a multi-class learner or not.

Initate Machine Learning

Once the data was cleanded and prepped we attempted to determine the best model for the data without over/underfitting. Based on models tested, the Random Forest Method generated the best results.

Deployment

Utilized Pickle to download the Machine Learning model and create an instance that runs in a webapp connected to Heroku and can generate predictions based on unser input.
